Frequently asked questions

How does the custom model training work?

You can upload your company documentation, past support tickets, or website URLs. Our system processes this data to contextually train the AI, ensuring it answers specific questions about your business accurately.

Can I integrate this with my existing web app?

Yes! We provide easy-to-use SDKs for React, Vue, Python, and Node.js, along with a standard REST API that can be integrated into any tech stack.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. We are SOC2 compliant, and your proprietary data is never used to train global public models.
